The Rezon Story

Like any great story starts with a tragedy, this is ours. We were and are tired of seeing brands and websites that just look rip off each other, trying to be the new apple or any other popular brand.

Seeing design agencies that just either creating copy-paste designs or on other extremes creating fancy cool looking stuff just for the sake of looking fancy with little to no actual value without understanding about your need and problems and complete disregard to your audience.

To change this Rezon studio is born. We want people to be original by helping them put their original selves out there and speaking to their audience in ‘their language’. We want people to own their story instead of narrating someone else, and having brands and websites that really convey what they mean to their valuable audience.

How Rezon got its name?

Rezon is a misspelling of the word reason. We knew who we are, and we want the world to know us by the same. And what could be better than ‘Rezon’ for a brand (us) that is driven by reasoning and purpose.
